Chief Engineer

At our core, we believe in building a stronger future with innovation and integrity. Our team is led by Mr. Arun S, a highly skilled Structural Engineer with over 6 years of hands-on experience in civil and structural design.

Arun holds an M.Tech in Structural Engineering and has played a key role in designing high-rise RCC and composite buildings across major projects including Cyberthum Noida, Max Estate Gurgaon, and several government infrastructures like DMRC Simulator Building and AIIMS extensions.

He is an expert in advanced design tools like CSI ETABS, SAFE, AutoCAD, and STAAD Pro, ensuring all projects meet the highest safety and efficiency standards. With a creative mindset and a problem-solving approach, he continues to lead our engineering vision with passion and precision.

About Tool with Tricks

Tool with Tricks was founded and is based in Schaan, Liechtenstein. Our team members around the world contribute to making construction work more productive, safer, and more sustainable. We do this with our hardware, software and service offering.
